Maddy and Lily's Cross Country Road Trip

Over the summer, Lily and I did a cross-country road trip. Lily is from California. We drove her car to get it back to school in Vermont! Underneath are all the stops we took throughout the trip.

  • Dana Point, California (Lily's Hometown!)
  • Grand Canyon
  • Durango, Colorado
  • Denver, Colorado
  • Kansas University
  • Chicago, Illinois
  • Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • Guilford, Connecticut (My Hometown!)
  • University of Vermont (Back to School!)

Here's a look into how long our drive was...

Stops Time
Dana Point, CA to Grand Canyon 7 hr 35 min
Grand Canyon to Durango, CO 5 hr 25 min
Durango, CO to Denver, CO 7 hr (Took Route 550)
Denver, CO to KU 7 hr 45 min
KU to Chicago, IL 7 hr 30 min
Chicago, IL to Pittsburgh, PA 7 hr
Pittsburgh, PA to Guilford, CT 7 hr 30 min
Guilford, CT to UVM 4 hr
